Vi (Vim):
Navigation:
h, j, k, l: Move left, down, up, right respectively.
w: Move forward to the beginning of the next word.
b: Move backward to the beginning of the previous word.
e: Move forward to the end of the current word.
0: Move to the beginning of the line.
^: Move to the first non-blank character of the line.
$: Move to the end of the line.
H, M, L: Move to the top, middle, or bottom line of the screen.
Ctrl+f: Page down.
Ctrl+b: Page up.
G: Move to the end of the file.
gg: Move to the beginning of the file.
<line_number>G: Move to a specific line number.
Saving Changes:
:w: Save changes.
:w filename: Save as filename.
:q: Quit without saving.
:q!: Quit and discard changes.
:wq or ZZ: Save changes and quit.
Other Tasks:
i: Insert mode.
x: Delete character under cursor.
dd: Delete current line.
yy: Copy current line.
p: Paste copied or deleted text.
/pattern: Search for pattern.
Emacs:
Navigation:
Ctrl+p, Ctrl+n: Move up and down.
Ctrl+f, Ctrl+b: Move forward and backward.
Ctrl+a: Move to the beginning of the line.
Ctrl+e: Move to the end of the line.
Alt+<: Move to the beginning of the buffer.
Alt+>: Move to the end of the buffer.
Ctrl+v: Page down.
Meta+v: Page up.
Saving Changes:
Ctrl+x Ctrl+s: Save changes.
Ctrl+x Ctrl+c: Quit Emacs.
Other Tasks:
Ctrl+g: Cancel command.
Ctrl+k: Delete from cursor to end of line.
Ctrl+y: Yank (paste) deleted text.
Ctrl+s: Search forward.
Ctrl+r: Search backward.
Nano:
Navigation:
Arrow Keys: Navigate up, down, left, and right.
Ctrl+f: Move forward one character.
Ctrl+b: Move backward one character.
Alt+f: Move forward one word.
Alt+b: Move backward one word.
Ctrl+a: Move to the beginning of the line.
Ctrl+e: Move to the end of the line.
Ctrl+v: Page down.
Ctrl+y: Page up.
Alt+\: Move to the first line.
Alt+/: Move to the last line.
Saving Changes:
Ctrl+o: WriteOut (save changes).
Ctrl+x: Exit (quit).
Other Tasks:
Ctrl+k: Cut (delete) text.
Ctrl+u: Uncut (paste) text.
Ctrl+_: Go to a specific line number.
